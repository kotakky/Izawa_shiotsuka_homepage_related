Personal Mobility and Location Based Services
Pedestrian Self-localization Using Mobile Devices 2012~
Pedestrian dead reckoning (PDR) method detects walking action such as step size and direction and cumulates relative movements. Integrating PDR with GPS, we realize more accurate pedestrian self-localization.
Self-localization using Wi-Fi Fingerprint technology 2015~
For the calibration of WiFi-Fingerprinting, GNSS measurements are usually used as reference data. We use our 3D-GNSS method for the calibration and aim to improve WiFI-fingerprinting accuracy.
Pedestrian Navigation utilizing Wearable Devices 2016~
We use our pedestrian self-localization approach and image matching technique on "Eyeglass-type wearable device" to realize more accurate pedestrian self-localization. In addition, we use AR technology and aim to provide new navigation experience.

Autonomous Driving
Self-localization
For autonomous driving, ITS and smart-phone applications, it is significant to know the accurate self location. We focus on self-localization in urban canyons and are aiming to establish self-localization methods with high accuracy utilizing GNSS such as GPS and on-board/smart-phone sensors such as an inertial sensor.
GNSS Error Correction Using 3D Maps in Urban Canyons 2012~
Using 3D building map and ray-tracing simulation, our method correct multipath and non-line-of-sight (NLOS) propagation which are main error factors in urban canyons. In addition, we realize more accurate positioning using Multi-GNSS and L1-SAIF signal information.
GNSS Positioning Correction Method Using Altitude Map 2014~
In GNSS measurement, there is strong correlation between vertical errors and horizontal errors. With altitude maps, we estimate vertical errors and correct horizontal error based on the vertical errors.
Sensor Integration for Vehicle Self-Localization in Urban Environment 2014~
By integrating on-board sensors such as speed sensor and gyro sensor with GNSS measurement, we realize high-precision vehicle self-localization.

Scenario Understanding
For autonomous driving and Advanced Driving Assistant Systems (ADAS), it is essential to maintain safety for pedestrian and bicyclist. We approach various layers from pedestrian detection to behavior recognition for this theme.
Pedestrian Detection by On-board Vision Sensor
Combining object detection by our
Spatio-Temporal MRF model (ST-MRF) and classification algorithms using HOG feature etc., our system extract pedestrians from on-board camera images.
Recognition and Pose Estimation of Urban Road Users from On-board Cameras
We propose behavior analysis framework which classify pedestrian and bicyclist and estimate head and body orientations which represent the direction of attention and movement using HOG, SVM and constraints.
Pedestrian Detection Using RSSI 2011~2015
From Received Signal Strength Indicator (RSSI) of V2P communication, our system estimate pedestrian positions relative to the vehicle.
Pedestrian Behavior Recognition at Signalized Intersections 2014~
With a probabilistic model considering contexts in signalized intersections, our system recognize pedestrian intention to cross crosswalks.

3D Map
3D maps are indispensable for self-localization technology such as camera and laser based ones and our 3D-GNSS. Accuracy of the maps affects on results of the self-localization. We are working on 3D map generation and correction methods.
3D Building Map Reconstruction by Integrating Point Cloud with 2D Map 2014~
We are developing methods to extract building boundaries from huge point cloud data by MMS and generate 3D map consisting of necessary and sufficient information.
Correction of Building Footprint for 3D City Models using GNSS Measurement 2014~
We propose a method for correcting building footprints in 3D maps utilizing our 3D-GNSS measurement system.

Traffic Infrastructure
Traffic Surveillance
In recent years, a lot of surveillance cameras are set in road environment and traffic flow measurements and traffic event detections are conducted. We have carried out researches for technologies which automatically extract intended information without human work.
Traffic Analysis System
We developed systems which automatically obtain information such as average speed and traffic density by our tracking algorithm robust to occlusion.
Rule Based Real-time Event Detection System
We developed systems which detect traffic events such as accidents, jam and falling objects from image sequences.

Traffic Signal Control
In the conventional traffic signal control, parameters such as cycle length and split are fixed. It causes unnecessary waiting time in off-hours. We conducted research on road vehicle cooperated signal control system.
Intersection Signal Control Employing Vision Sensors
We proposed that our advanced vision sensor which aggregates vehicle/pedestrian measurement functions is effective for optimization of pedestrian signal splits.

Surveillance Camera
Recently, a huge number of surveillance cameras are set in stations and retail stores for the purpose of security and marketing. However, there is a limit to keep watching and analyze by hand. We are carrying out researches for automatic processing method for surveillance camera images.
Understanding Pedestrian Behavior in Railway Station 2010~2013
We constructed a behavior understanding framework in railway stations by combining object position and orientation.
Customer Pose Recognition for Retail Surveillance 2012~2013
We proposed a customer pose estimation method from surveillance cameras in retail stores.
Customer Behavior Recognition in Retail Store 2014~
We are doing research on a method which recognizes interest and behavior to goods from surveillance

Marketing Support
Coustomer Behavior
Recently, there has been an increased demand for understanding the behavior and position of people within large-scale indoor facilities. In this research, we propose an low-cost system to determine a person's activity and indoor positioning using only smartphone sensors (accelerometer and gyroscope).

Coustomer Interest
Conventionally, retailers hire human labors or consulting companies to watch the surveillance video and extract useful marketing information. The video analyzers can assess how interested in the merchandise the customers are by observing their behaviors.
Estimation of Customer Interest Level Using Surveillance Camera for Marketing 2015~
We constructed a visual system to recognize customer action and interest during the shopping process
